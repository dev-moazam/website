About us

I have more than 3 years of experience as a Head of Effluent Treatment Plant at Kohinoor Mills Limited. As per design capacity, plant can treat maximum up to 150 m3/hr or 3600 m3/day. Salt and dyes laden high strength wastewater is treated biological and chemical treatment process thereby meeting the local (Pakistan Environmental Quality Standards) and more stringent international discharge guidelines (Zero Discharge of Hazardous Chemical Wastewater Guidelines). To address the issue of water scarcity, a two stage Reverse Osmosis plant have been installed after secondary treatment to reduce the TDS level within acceptable range as required for textile production process.
